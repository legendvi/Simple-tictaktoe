Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of banglades who is especiallly recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, msician and philosopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writing deai with the revolotion of that period of the British goverment. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised hisvoice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got opularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, jumanity, and revolution. Hes opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamaentalism , including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his carer, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but best known for his songs and poems. He poineered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs (many recorded HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
